Missionary Department

The Missionary Department is responsible for fulfilling the Great Commission of Jesus Christ by spreading the Gospel to the unreached people. It serves as the outreach arm of the FPC focusing on evangelism, church planting, discipleship, and social impact in unreached or underserved areas.

  1. Monthly Financial Support: In the state of Jammu and Kashmir, 15 missionaries have been selected and are being supported through FPC Kanyakumari District with a monthly contribution of ₹5,000 each (totaling ₹75,000 per month).
  2. Missionary Visits: Once every quarter, a missionary team travels to Jammu and Kashmir to visit and encourage the missionaries serving there.
  3. Annual Mission Conference: Every year, through the FPC Missionary Department of Kanyakumari District, a missionary vision conference (Missionary Darisanam GMD) is organized in the district.
  4. Monthly Planning and Prayer Meetings: The FPC Missionary Committee gathers once a month to pray for the missionaries and to plan mission strategies and activities.
  5. Regional Collaboration: With the cooperation of pastors from 8 regional zones under FPC Kanyakumari District, missionary outreach and related ministries are being actively carried out.
