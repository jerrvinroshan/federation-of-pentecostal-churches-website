Prayer Co-ordination Department

The Prayer Coordination Department is a vital spiritual unit within the FPC that focuses on cultivating a strong foundation of prayer.

Through the FPC Prayer Coordination Committee, a prayer meeting is held on the first Wednesday of every month from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM, where the prayer coordinators and preachers gather to pray.

Its primary responsibilities include:

  • Intercession:Praying consistently for the church, its leadership, members, and the surrounding community.
  • Organizing Prayer Meetings: Planning and conducting regular prayer gatherings, special prayer events, and seasonal fasting and prayer sessions.
  • Training and Equipping: Teaching pastors and believers on the importance of prayer, and equipping them with practical tools and biblical principles to develop an effective prayer life.
  • Spiritual Atmosphere: Creating an environment where prayer is central to every aspect of church life and ministry.
  • Spiritual Warfare: Providing guidance and training on engaging in spiritual warfare based on Scripture, empowering members to stand firm in faith and victory

Every month, various prayer activities are conducted in different regions, such as:

  • Night prayers
  • Zonal level All-night prayers
  • Fasting prayers for ministry workers and their families
  • Prayer camps
  • Prayer walks
  • Prayer Drive across the entire district
    • These prayer initiatives continue to take place regularly.
