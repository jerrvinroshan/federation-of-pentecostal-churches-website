About us

During the period when Pentecostal ministries were growing in Kanyakumari district, in the year 1984, according to the vision given by the Lord to Pr. Y.S. Devasundaram, other servants of God Pastor N Thomas, Pastor Isaac Christian gathered together in prayer and formed a fellowship called ("Deva Uzhiayar Aikkiyam Njt Copah; If;fpak;) " (Pastors Fellowship) to help unite Pentecostal ministers in prayer and fellowship. The Lord, who initiated this fellowship, has graciously continued it to this day.

The organization, which had been actively functioning, continued to grow and, in 3.10.2006 , with the aim of including believers as well, it was registered as a society under the name "Federation of Pentecostal Churches." at Nagerocil. It was established with the following 17 leaders, with Pastor Dhasisah(Kirubasam Church) and Pastor Mariadhas(Jehovah Church) serving as witnesses.

The transition from the Pastors Fellowship to the Federation of Pentecostal Churches (FPC) marked a key moment of structural and organizational development for the group. With this shift, the leadership also expanded, with several key individuals taking on roles that would help steer the vision and growth of the FPC. This leadership structure suggests a well-organized initiative aimed at fostering growth, collaboration, and deeper spiritual engagement within the Pentecostal community.

To take this vision across all of Tamil Nadu, Pastor Y.S. Devasundaram, along with Pastor J.J.Y from Madurai, took the initiative. What began as a fellowship of churches and leaders in 18 districts Continuing the vision of Pastor Devasundaram and under divine guidance and leadership, the Synod of Pentecostal Churches was officially registered as a trust in Chennai in 2008, under the leadership of Pastor Owen Roberts which includes Church leaders from Chennai to Kanyakumari.
